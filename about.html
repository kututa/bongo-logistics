Our History

Since our inception in 2005, Bongo Express Cargo has grown from a local delivery service to an international logistics powerhouse, focusing on innovative solutions for air, sea, and ground shipping.

Our commitment to excellence in cargo handling, timely delivery, and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred choice for businesses across Africa and beyond.
